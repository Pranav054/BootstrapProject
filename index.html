Puran Poli is a traditional Indian flatbread, predominantly relished in Maharashtra. The name 'Puran Poli' is a blend of two Marathi words: 'Puran,'referring to the sweet lentil filling, and 'Poli,' which is the soft, thin flatbread enveloping the stuffing. The essence of Puran Poli lies in the balance it achieves - the sweet lentil stuffing harmoniously mingling with the wheat flour dough, creating a perfect amalgamation of flavours.
